About the Project

BuktransPLANt is a modern web-based agricultural service management platform designed to streamline the booking and operational processes of mechanized rice transplanting services in Bukidnon. Rather than functioning as a simple booking website, the platform serves as the company's centralized management system, connecting farmers, operators, branch administrators, and system administrators within a single ecosystem.

The platform enables farmers to conveniently book mechanized rice transplanting services, choose service packages, schedule operations, monitor booking progress, make online payments, and receive digital completion reports. On the operational side, it provides tools for managing machine fleets, operator assignments, nursery production, branch scheduling, customer records, payments, and business analytics.

The primary goal of BuktransPLANt is to modernize agricultural service delivery through digital transformation by improving efficiency, reducing manual coordination, and providing a seamless experience for both customers and company personnel. By integrating booking management, fleet operations, scheduling, and future AI-powered features into one platform, BuktransPLANt aims to become a scalable, enterprise-grade solution that supports smarter farming and precision agricultural services.
